About Luna Café

Our Story

Luna Café was founded in 2025 by three passionate creators — Mursib, Ishaan, and Jason. What began as a shared love for quality coffee and meaningful spaces has grown into a warm, welcoming café focused on community, comfort, and craftsmanship.

Our Mission

At Luna Café, we strive to bring people together through exceptional coffee, locally sourced ingredients, and an inviting environment where everyone feels at home. Whether you’re stopping by for your morning boost or a peaceful evening drink, we’re here to make every visit special.

Why “Luna”?

Inspired by the calm and beauty of the moon, Luna Café represents a place where guests can unwind, recharge, and enjoy moments of quiet reflection. Just like the moonlight, our café aims to bring a sense of calm and brightness to your day.
